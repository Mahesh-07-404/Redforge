# SAFETY: Legal & Compliance

Ensure all security assessments adhere to safe harbor terms, legal frameworks, and ethical disclosure protocols.

## Compliance Frameworks
- **Safe Harbor**: Follow policy constraints to receive authorization protection.
- **Reporting Standards**: Document and report findings only through designated secure disclosure channels.
- **Ethics**: Do not cause service disruption (DoS), perform social engineering, or test physical security unless explicitly authorized.
- **Incident Response**: If a system is accidentally disrupted or data leakage is observed, stop testing immediately, document the state, and notify the program coordinator.
- **Responsible Disclosure**: Provide a complete, secure report to the vendor and allow reasonable mitigation time before public release.
